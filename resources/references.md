## Papers

### Foundational Papers

**Neural Networks:**
- "Learning representations by back-propagating errors" (Rumelhart et al., 1986)
- "Deep Learning" (LeCun, Bengio, Hinton, 2015) - Nature review

**CNNs:**
- "ImageNet Classification with Deep CNNs" (Krizhevsky et al., 2012) - AlexNet
- "Deep Residual Learning" (He et al., 2015) - ResNet

**RNNs/LSTMs:**
- "Long Short-Term Memory" (Hochreiter & Schmidhuber, 1997)
- "Sequence to Sequence Learning" (Sutskever et al., 2014)

**Transformers:**
- "Attention Is All You Need" (Vaswani et al., 2017)
- "BERT" (Devlin et al., 2018)
- "GPT-3" (Brown et al., 2020)

**Generative Models:**
- "Generative Adversarial Networks" (Goodfellow et al., 2014)
- "Auto-Encoding Variational Bayes" (Kingma & Welling, 2013)
- "Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models" (Ho et al., 2020)

## Tips for Continued Learning

1. **Build projects** - Apply what you learn

4. **Teach others** - Best way to solidify knowledge

7. **Follow researchers** - Twitter, blogs
8. **Experiment** - Try new models and techniques
